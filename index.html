Emotion Recongintion System

    The Emotion recognition System software is to develop a device which could appropriately detect feelings from both visible (facial) and auditory (speech) inputs. The gadget supports the following features:
  • Facial Emotion Recognition: Detecting emotions from images, videos, and live webcam feeds.
  • Speech Emotion Recognition: Detecting emotions from audio files and live audio.
  • Facial-Speech Emotion Recognition (FSER): A combined model that processes both visual and auditory inputs to provide a holistic emotion analysis for a video.

Student Registration Form

    The form serves as a structured way to gather essential information from potential students. This data is then used for various administrative purposes, such as:
  • Creating student records: The information is used to populate a database for student management.
  • Verification: Date of birth and address may be used for verifying the student's identity.
  • Communication: Email and mobile number are used to contact students.
  • Enrollment: The data is used to process the student's enrollment in the institution or program.
  • Demographic analysis: The collected data can be used to analyze student demographics for planning and improvement purposes.
